How can we help you?

    How can I order ?

A: You can order easily using our online platform. When you find a product you need, you can add it to cart, login and go through the ordering process. After the order is ready, you will receive order summary to your email. Order summary will also be stored to your account. You can also easily make reorders afterwards by clicking the “reorder” button on any of your previously made orders. After clicking the “reorder” button the cart will open and you can change quantities or products.

    Why should I buy online?

A: Speeding up the process. By ordering online you will you will get prices faster and you will be able to go through order confirmation and payment process much faster. This could save days of your time. Traceability: You will have easy access to all of your previous orders any time you want. Reordering: you can make a re-order anytime based on your previous orders by only couple of clicks. This will save time and effort as you don’t need to go through all the documents and emails from the past.

    What information should I input when ordering?

A: our online ordering system will ask for all the important information you should submit. If you have a VAT number, please remember to submit it. This will make sure the shipment is not delayed because of the lack of VAT number

    What payment methods can I use?

A: You can use all the major credit cards. Paying methods like GOGGLE PAY and PHONE PAY are also supported formats.

    What should I do if the payment is not accepted?

A: Please try again in a little while. If the payment is still not accepted, please verify your account balance. If everything is as it should, but you still can't make the payment, please contact hytest@hytest.fi notify us about the problem. We can manage the order manually.

    How can I change delivery address?

A: Sign in to your account and go to “my account”. On “my account” you can change all your contact information.

    What are the delivery charges?

A: Delivery charges are dependent on the shipment requirements. If the products on your order are due to special requirements (for example dry ice) extra fee will be added to the shipment charges. You can see the shipping fees on the checkout process before the payment is made.

    Do you have the product in stock?

A: All the products which are shown on our site are available. Order lead time depends on the products and quantities.

    How do create an account?

A: Go to the login page and provide the further details.

    How can I retrieve my password?

A: You can retrieve your password by clicking “forgot password?”. Instruction on password retrieval will be send to your email.

    Can I track my order?

A: We will send you the tracking code of the shipment when the parcel has been sent.

    Shipping time?

A: Shipping time will be confirmed on the order confirmation document.

    Shipping cost?

A: No shipping costs will be needed on the time of delivery.

    Changing the shipping address?

A: You can easily change your shipping address on your account. You just need to login and click “my account” and “edit”. You can also change the shipping address during the checkout process if you need. Just click “edit” below “shipping address”.
